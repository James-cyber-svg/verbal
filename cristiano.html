About

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, speed, and athleticism, Ronaldo has played for top clubs such as Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and the Portugal national team.

Achievements

Throughout his career, Ronaldo has won numerous titles, including 5 Ballon d'Or awards, 5 Champions League trophies, and 7 league titles across different countries. He is also the all-time top scorer for both Portugal and Real Madrid and will hopefully reach 1000 goals soon.

Ballon d'Ors

Ronaldo has won many Ballon d'Or awards, with his first one in 2008. That year, he was phenomenal, winning every single individual trophy and his first Champions League. His next Ballon d'Ors came in 2013, 2014, 2016, and 2017.
